ZIPLOCK BAGS


As a leading manufacturer in the resealable packaging market, everplastics® has developed a know-how in the reclosable flexible bag business. With over 60 years of expertise in plastic processing industry, we are at the forefront of product innovation, able to convent and optimise our manufacturing processes to offer you innovative and custom-made reclosable flexible bags.
  • More than a simple opening and closing function expected by consumers, the zip lock bag brings a real added value to the packaging.
  • The zipper prevents product loss, protects from contamination and gives your brand high visibility.
  • Integrated or applied to the bag, a wide range of bag designs is possible with a choice of materials, closures, seals and printing.
